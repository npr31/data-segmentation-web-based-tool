Gain Chart

What is a Gain Chart?
The gain chart shows how many positive responses (like customer conversions) you can capture by targeting a certain percentage of your customers, ranked by the model's predictions. A steeper curve means the model is good at finding the best customers first. For example, if you target the top 50% of customers (as ranked by the model), you can capture {{ stats.gain_at_50 }}% of all positive responses.

At 50% targeting: {{ stats.gain_at_50 }}% customers captured

Lift Chart

What is a Lift Chart?
The lift chart compares how much better your model is at finding positive responses compared to random targeting. A higher lift means your model is much better than guessing. For example, a lift of {{ stats.lift_at_50 }}x at 50% means you are {{ stats.lift_at_50 }} times more likely to find positive responses by using the model than by picking customers at random.

At 50% targeting: {{ stats.lift_at_50 }}x improvement
